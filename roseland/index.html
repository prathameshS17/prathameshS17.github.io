Welcome to Roseland
Welcome to Roseland English Medium High School — where learning meets purpose! Established in 1974 by the visionary educationist Mrs. Daulat Khimani, Roseland stands as the only institution in Jalgaon offering quality English Medium Education at an affordable cost, ensuring that every child gets the opportunity to learn, grow, and excel.
For over five decades, Roseland has been more than just a school — it's been a family of learners, dreamers, and achievers. Our mission is to shape young minds with excellence, ethics, and empathy, preparing them to thrive in a global world while staying rooted in Indian values.
At Roseland, we believe education should inspire curiosity and creativity. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced and caring faculty, and innovative teaching practices provide a nurturing environment where every child's potential is discovered and celebrated.

About Us
Building a legacy of excellence since 1974
Our Legacy
Founded in 1974, Roseland English Medium High School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Jalgaon for over five decades.
Our founder, Mrs. Daulat Khimani, envisioned a school that would empower children with English-medium education and values that last a lifetime.
Today, Roseland continues to inspire thousands of students, balancing academic excellence with character-building and life skills.
Our Vision
To be a leading institution that empowers every child with quality education, strong values, and global competencies.
We envision creating well-rounded individuals who are not only academically excellent but also confident, compassionate, and prepared to make a positive impact in the world.
Our Mission
To provide accessible, affordable, and quality English-medium education that nurtures curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking.
We are committed to holistic development through modern facilities, experienced faculty, and innovative teaching methodologies aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP).

Rose Town Preschool
Our pre-primary program focuses on play-based learning to nurture curiosity and a love for learning from an early age.
Children are introduced to phonics, numbers, colors, and shapes through interactive activities, storytelling, and creative play.
The emphasis is on building social skills, confidence, and a strong foundation for lifelong learning.
